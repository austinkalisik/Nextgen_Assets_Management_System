System Overview
- This is a Laravel + ReactJS CRUD application
- It is an asset management and office operations system
- It is owned by Nextgen Technology
- It is intended for Nextgen Technology and can also be used by other departments if adopted

Technology Structure
- Laravel backend
- ReactJS frontend
- API-driven CRUD workflows
- Database-backed modules for assets, assignments, suppliers, users, and settings

Current ERD Alignment Notes
- graph.png was regenerated from the live project database
- The generated ERD shows the modeled operational entities used by the app
