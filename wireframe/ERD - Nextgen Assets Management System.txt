Current Operational Entities In The UI
- Item: inventory item or asset record.
- Department: office/business unit used for reports and receiver grouping.
- Receiver: person/team receiving assets. This belongs to a department in current app behavior.
- Assignment: issue/return record for item movement.
- StockMovement: quantity change history.
- AssetLog: audit trail.
- SystemNotification: alerts and workflow messages.
- User: login account and role holder.
- Category and Supplier: inventory organization.

Relationship Summary in the Generated ERD
- Category has many Items.
- Supplier has many Items.
- Item belongs to Category.
- Item belongs to Supplier.
- Item has many Assignments.
- Item has many active Assignments.
- Item has one active Assignment.
- Item has many AssetLogs.
- Item has many StockMovements.
- Assignment belongs to Item.
- Assignment belongs to User.
- Assignment belongs to Department through department_id in current app logic.
- Department has many Assignments.
- Department has many active Assignments.
- AssetLog belongs to Item.
- AssetLog belongs to User.
- StockMovement belongs to Item.
- StockMovement belongs to Supplier.
- StockMovement belongs to User.
- User has many Assignments.
- User has many active Assignments.
- User has many AssetLogs.
- User has many SystemNotifications.
- SystemNotification belongs to User.

Current Department-First Assignment Meaning
- Departments are the reporting group.
- Receivers are selected inside their department context.
- Giving out an asset should be read as:
  Item -> Department / Person Receiving -> Quantity -> Assignment record.
- Reports should make it easy to answer:
  Which department has this item?
  Which person in that department has it?
  How many are still issued out?

Inventory Tracking Meaning
- Bulk stock: one item row can hold many units.
- Serialized asset: one unique asset, normally one unit, identified by serial number and/or asset tag.

Depreciation Meaning
- Depreciation fields support fixed-asset valuation and book-value calculations.
- Depreciation Start Date and Purchase Date are date-only fields in YYYY-MM-DD format.

Operational Data Flow
1. User logs in.
2. Item records are created and updated.
3. StockMovement records opening stock, stock in, stock out, return, transfer, and adjustment.
4. Receiver belongs to Department.
5. Assignment records item, department/person receiving, quantity, assigned date, and returned date.
6. AssetLog records audit actions.
7. SystemNotification records alerts and workflow notifications.
8. Activity Logs and Assignment reports can export filtered CSV/PDF outputs.
